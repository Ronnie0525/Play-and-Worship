Welcome to Play & Worship

Your modern worship presentation console — songs, scripture, media, and a live projector window, all in your browser. Here's the quick tour.

1

Open the projector

Click the green Live Output button in the top-right toolbar. A second window opens — drag it to your projector monitor and press F for fullscreen.

2

Build your service

Use the Library on the left to pick songs, scripture verses, or media. Double-click an item to add it to your schedule, or press Add.

3

Preview, then go live

Click a schedule card to preview its slides in the right panel. Press Space or click Go Live to push the selected slide to the projector.

4

Keyboard shortcuts

←/→ navigate slides · B blackout · Esc clear · F1 countdown. Full list under the Shortcuts menu.
