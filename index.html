Feature 1 / 7 · Introduction

Everything you need on a Sunday morning.

Built for operators who want less clicking and more worshipping.

Songs & Hymns

Full-featured editor with auto slide splitting, chord/CCLI metadata, themeable backgrounds, and an English / Tagalog language filter.

Scripture

All 66 books across 5 translations (WEB, KJV, ASV, YLT, BBE). Jump-to-reference search, paginated long passages, colored verse numbers, and live edit of font size, text color, and background — every scripture slide updates instantly.

Media Library

Import PPTX, PDF, video, and images (JPG / PNG / WEBP / GIF). Stored locally in IndexedDB — your full library survives a browser reload, no uploads required.

Live Projector

Dedicated output window for the sanctuary display. Drag to the secondary monitor, press F for fullscreen. Smooth motion backgrounds, blackout, and countdown — all one keypress away.

Service Schedule

Build your service order, drag to reorder, double-click to go live. Go Live routes to whatever you just clicked — slide, verse, image, or video — and arrow keys stay inside the current item so a stray tap never jumps to the next song.

No Install · Keyboard-First

Runs in any modern browser. Songs, media, settings, and schedule stay on your device — no uploads, no tracking. Quick keys: N new song, I import, S / H / B / M switch tabs, F1 countdown, Ctrl+S confirms auto-save.

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before Sunday morning.

How do I get an account?

Request a license via the Pricing section. Each license unlocks one operator account for the web app and (soon) the desktop build. Your sign-in credentials are delivered once the license is activated.

Why is Play & Worship a paid tool?

Every license directly supports the ministries behind Play & Worship and funds ongoing development — new features, updates, and operator support. It's a small investment that keeps the tool alive for the churches that depend on it every Sunday.

Do I need an internet connection to use it?

Only for the Scripture browser (which fetches verses from a trusted Bible source). Everything else — songs, media, PPTX/PDF/images, videos, the projector — runs fully offline. Your full library is stored locally in IndexedDB, so it's still there after a browser reload.

How do I run it on a second screen / projector?

Click Live Output in the toolbar. A dedicated projector window opens that you drag to your second display, then press F for fullscreen. The operator console stays on your laptop — the congregation only sees the projector window.

Does it support PowerPoint, PDF, video, and images?

Yes to all four. PPTX shows the first image + text per slide (best for simple decks); for pixel-perfect rendering, export to PDF first. Video clips play directly on the projector with loop/mute controls. Images (JPG / PNG / WEBP / GIF / BMP) are added as single-slide decks that you can drop straight into your schedule.

Can I style scripture passages?

Yes — open a scripture item in the workspace and use the three buttons next to Verses / slide: font size, text color, and background color. Changes save to that passage instantly and push to the projector if it's live. Verse numbers are highlighted in gold by default so the congregation can easily track reference points in multi-verse slides.

Can I import songs from another tool?

Use Import Song and paste lyrics with [Verse 1] / [Chorus] markers. Play & Worship auto-detects section headers and splits the song into slide-ready chunks. Plain lyrics without markers work too — blank lines become new slides.

Do you offer ministry or multi-site pricing?

Yes. Reach out via the Pricing section with a short note about your context — smaller congregations and church networks have flexible options available.
